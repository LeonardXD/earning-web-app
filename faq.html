Frequently Asked Questions

What is DemoTasker - Earn?

DemoTasker - Earn is a platform where you can earn virtual coins by solving simple math equations, captchas, and claiming daily rewards. These coins can then be used within the platform for various purposes.

How do I earn coins?

You can earn coins by:

  • Solving math equations: Each correct answer rewards you with coins.
  • Solving captchas: Similar to equations, correct captcha entries earn you coins.
  • Claiming daily rewards: Log in daily to claim a bonus amount of coins.

Is there a limit to how many equations/captchas I can solve?

No, there's no limit — you can solve as much as you want.

What are daily tasks?

Daily tasks are additional challenges, such as solving a certain number of equations or captchas, that offer extra coin rewards upon completion. You can view your progress and claim rewards from the "Daily Tasks" modal.

How do I claim my daily reward?

Navigate to the "Daily Reward" mode on the main page and click the "Claim Reward" button. You can claim this reward once every 24 hours.

What can I do with the coins I earn?

Currently, coins are used within the platform for various features. More functionalities and ways to utilize your coins will be added in future updates.

How do I withdraw?

To withdraw your earnings, go to the Contact section and send us a message on our Facebook page.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The minimum withdrawal amount is 1,000 coins, which is equal to 1 PHP.
